Why Choose PBIS

Experience Education
That Inspires

A campus tour is the best way to truly understand what makes PBIS exceptional. Come and see for yourself.

British Curriculum
Delivering the National Curriculum of England from Nursery through to Year 13. Accredited by COBIS and FOBISIA. IGCSE and A-Level qualifications recognised worldwide.
International Community
A truly diverse community of students, families and educators from over 30 nationalities. We celebrate cultural richness while delivering British academic excellence.
Holistic Development
Our approach nurtures academic achievement alongside personal growth, creativity, sport, and leadership — developing well-rounded individuals prepared for a global future.
Modern Facilities
State-of-the-art classrooms, science labs, libraries, swimming pools, sports fields and dedicated arts spaces — all designed to inspire curiosity and learning.
Experienced Educators
Our teaching team comprises qualified British and international teachers with years of experience delivering the UK curriculum in international settings.
Vientiane's Premier School
Established in 2001, PBIS is Vientiane's most established British international school with three convenient campuses serving families across the capital.
